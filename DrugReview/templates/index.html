USA - Drug Review Data Analysis and Sentment Prediction


About this project!

Given the importance of proper drugs to cure health conditions, our team has developed a application using machine learning and natural language processing in an attempt to identify sentiment from the review and rating provided by the user for various drugs. This application, also enables anyone visiting our website to find out top medicines used for various health conditions, based on the collected drug review data, using visualizations and interactive charts along with many anaswers to many other questions you may have....






Team members: Nilam Roy and Indu
